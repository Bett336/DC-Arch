Welcome to DC Architects, your trusted partner in innovative architectural design.

DC Architects was founded in 1987 by visionary architect and entrepreneur, David Cunningham. David, a Melbourne native, had a deep passion for transforming architectural dreams into tangible, awe-inspiring structures. Armed with a degree in Architecture and a relentless commitment to innovation, he set out to establish a firm that would redefine the standards of architectural excellence.

Our dedicated team of 200 professionals has been transforming dreams into reality since 1987.
